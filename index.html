Introduction of Game:

Persona 5 is a role-playing game developed by Atlas for the current generation of consoles(Xbox One/PS4). It is a JRPG which involves real-world issues but tackled in their own stylistic way through their use of gameplay and U.I that generally goes beyond most principles of U.I design and if compared to the interfaces of most modern video games, Persona stands out because of its individuality artistically and gameplay-wise. In terms of gameplay, the game takes on the darkest of subjects yet portrays them in-game in a light-hearted way with its reliance on comedy and characters and their depth which gets explored through dialogue, in true JRPG nature.
